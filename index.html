Biography

I am an Associate Research Scholar at the Department of Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering in Princeton University. Currently I am working with Prof. Naomi E. Leonard in the broad area of networked multi-agent systems. Please click here to know more specifics about my research. I also collaborate with Princeton Neuroscience Institute on a project about designing human inspired architecture for neural networks.

Before moving to Princeon in March 2015, I completed PhD in Electrical Engineering from University of Maryland, College Park. My dissertation on Reconstruction, Analysis and Synthesis of Collective Motion was supervised by Prof. P. S. Krishnaprasad. Earlier, I received Master of Technology (MTech) in Systems and Control Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, India in 2008. During my masters studies, I worked with Prof. Ravi N. Banavar in the area of infinite dimensional port-Hamiltonian systems and their stabilization. I earned Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University, India in 2006.
